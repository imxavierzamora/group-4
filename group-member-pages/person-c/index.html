About Me

I’m a bilingual data journalist based in the Bay Area and a first-year Master of Journalism student at the University of California, Berkeley.

I specialize in data-driven reporting and visualization, telling stories that uncover systems and patterns behind everyday life.

My recent reporting for Richmond Confidential explores city council pay and workload, the opioid-response system, and urban environmental equity.

Previously, I worked with The Paper (澎湃新闻) in Shanghai and co-founded an independent student data journalism team at the Communication University of China, where I collaborated on award-winning projects about public health and social issues..
